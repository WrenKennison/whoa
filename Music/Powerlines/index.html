It's like a strange, unintended, Walking Trees 2. . .

The main difference is that it's just better. Walking Trees sounds kinda strange, mainly because it was created with a "fix it in post" attitude so extreme it ended up being a "make it in post" situation. The spaced-out dreamy sound was a creative choice, but it also works overtime to hide a mountain of recording issues.
