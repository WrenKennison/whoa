Reciever



Receiver is a hot new Chicago-based 3-piece. With the Obliques' Jackson Steffens, Lepsius' Phoebe Johnson, and drummer Warren Glass, they have an unusual but ear-catching sound unlike any other.

Orgone Energy Accumulator



Orgone Energy Accumulator [also from durham] can be described in four words: loud, noise, industrial, and grating. Despite being described with the same section of the thesaurus usually reserved for rapidly failing washing machines, they are in fact strangely captivating, leaving you with ringing ears and a desire to hear more.

Gorilla and the Bricks
[in memoriam]



Gorilla and the Bricks were a short-lived band from our beloved Durham, NC. Only playing two shows, their one song, "jorking my peanits", is still talked about today. The Bricks are a testament to the truest act of musicianship: dicking around for fun.
